How it works

  1. 01
    Subscribe

    Send /start to @mayura4096_bot on Telegram.

  2. 02
    Choose your publications

    Use /prefs to toggle on or off any of the four publications.

  3. 03
    Receive, automatically

Under the hood

GitHub ActionsDaily scheduled scrapers download e-paper editions and compile PDFs.
Cloudflare R2PDFs are cached in object storage for fast, reliable delivery.
